# Case Closed

## Overview

Case Closed is a 3D detective mystery game developed in Unity. Players take on the role of a detective who becomes stranded at a secluded mansion after their car breaks down. What begins as an ordinary request for shelter quickly turns into a murder investigation.

The objective is to explore the mansion, collect evidence, question suspects, and identify the correct murderer, weapon, and motive before making a final accusation.

## Features

* 3D first-person investigation
* Randomized murderer each playthrough
* Randomized murder weapon and clue locations
* Interactive suspects with unique personalities and alibis
* Detective notebook for tracking evidence and witness statements
* Multiple endings based on the player's deduction
* AI-driven suspect schedules and behaviors

## Gameplay

Each new game generates a different mystery by selecting:

* The murderer
* The murder weapon
* The crime scene
* Various clue locations

Players must gather evidence, compare testimonies, inspect the crime scene, and solve the mystery before accusing a suspect.
